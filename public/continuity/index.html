Gameplay

Connect Every Trace

classic flow rules, better presentation

🔗

Draw Paths

Drag between same-colored endpoints. Every cell must be filled. Paths cannot cross — the grid is your canvas.

⭐

Perfect Clears

Solve in the minimum number of moves to earn a medal. They persist forever and show in the level select — chase mastery.

🧩

300 Verified Levels

From 5×5 warm-ups to brutal 10×10 endgame boards. Every level is procedurally generated and verified solvable by a 102-test suite.

📅

Daily Puzzle

A fresh challenge every day for 2 years. Build your streak, fill the calendar. Works fully offline — levels ship in the app.

🎨

Unlockable Themes

Four board themes unlock with progress: Neon (default), Frost, Ember, Void. Each reskins trace colors and cell styling.

🏆

Play Games + Cloud Save

12 achievements on Google Play Games. One-tap cloud save. Silent sign-in — no popups. Progress follows your account.
