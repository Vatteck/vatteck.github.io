// GAMEPLAY

How It Works

🔗

Connect the Dots

Drag between same-colored endpoints to create a path. Every cell must be filled. Paths cannot cross — plan carefully.

⭐

Perfect Clears

Solve a level in the minimum number of moves to earn a perfect-clear medal. They persist and show in the level select.

🧩

300 Hand-Generated Levels

From simple 5×5 warm-ups to brutal 10×10 endgame boards. Every level is solvable and uniquely solvable — verified by the generator test suite.

📅

Daily Puzzle

A fresh puzzle every day for 2 years. Build your streak, fill the calendar. Works offline — levels ship in the app, no server needed.

🎨

Unlockable Themes

Four board themes unlock as you progress: Neon (default), Frost (25 solves), Ember (100), Void (250). Reskins the board only — app chrome stays consistent.

🏆

Play Games Achievements

12 achievements via Google Play Games Services. Cloud save backs up your progress. Silent sign-in — no popups unless you want them.

// MONETIZATION

Fair & Unobtrusive

💡

Hint System

Stuck? Use a hint to reveal part of the solution path — always behind a confirmation dialog.

🎬

Rewarded Ads

Watch a short ad to earn a free hint. Opt-in only. Interstitials fire every 3rd completed level — infrequent enough to not annoy.

🛒

One-Time IAPs

Remove ads permanently. Or grab a hint pack. No subscriptions, no battle pass, no nonsense.
