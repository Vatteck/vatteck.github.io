Privacy Policy

Continuity - Last updated: July 24, 2026

1. What the App Itself Stores

Your campaign progress, perfect-clear medals, daily history and streak, hint balance, selected theme, preferences, and whether you have removed ads are stored on your device.

Continuity has no account system of its own. You can play without signing in to Play Games. If Play Games signs you in, the app can sync some progress as described below.

The optional daily reminder is scheduled on your device. Continuity does not use a notification server or send push notifications.

2. Google Play Games

Continuity uses Google Play Games Services for optional achievements and cloud save. When you are signed in, the app sends achievement unlocks to Google and stores the following game progress in one Play Games saved-game slot:

  • Campaign levels completed and completed perfectly
  • Daily puzzles completed and completed perfectly
  • Current and best daily streak information

Hint balance, selected theme, reminder preference, and Remove Ads ownership are not included in the cloud save. Remove Ads ownership is restored separately through Google Play Billing.

Google may associate Play Games activity and saved progress with your Play Games profile. Google Play services for Games may process gamer identity, account information that is discoverable to the service, IP address, country- or region-level location, device identifiers, game activity, achievements, saved progress, and diagnostic information to provide, secure, analyze, and improve Play Games.

Continuity does not receive your Google password or operate its own copy of your Google account profile. Google's handling of Play Games data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising

The app shows ads through Google AdMob. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share an IP address, which can be used to estimate general location; app interactions such as launches, taps, and video views; diagnostic and performance information; and device or account identifiers, including the Android advertising ID and app set ID. Google uses this information for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention.

In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the app asks for your consent before personalized ads are used; where consent is not given, ads are non-personalized.

Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy linked above. You can reset or delete your advertising identifier in your device settings.

Purchasing "Remove Ads" stops interstitial ads from being shown. Rewarded ads (which you choose to watch in exchange for hints) remain available.

4. Purchases

Purchases (Remove Ads and hint packs) are processed by Google Play Billing. The app receives confirmation that a purchase completed; it does not receive or store your payment details. Google's processing of purchases is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

The app does not sell your data. Data described above is shared only with the Google services and participating advertising providers needed to provide ads, process purchases, unlock Play Games achievements, and sync saved progress.

6. Your Controls

You can clear Continuity's local data through Android settings or by uninstalling the app. If you are signed in to Play Games, cloud progress can sync back to the device until you also delete Continuity's Play Games data.

You can manage automatic Play Games sign-in, your Play Games profile, and game-specific Play Games data in Google Play or Android settings. Google's instructions for deleting one game's data or a Play Games profile are available in Google Play Help.

Where required, Continuity provides Google's privacy-options form so you can review or change advertising consent. You can also reset or delete the Android advertising ID in device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is a general-audience puzzle game and is not directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new version will be posted at this URL.
