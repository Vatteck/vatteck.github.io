Privacy Policy

Continuity — Last updated: July 16, 2026

1. What the App Itself Stores

Your level progress, perfect-clear medals, hint balance, and whether you have removed ads are stored only on your device. The app has no account system and does not upload this information anywhere.

2. Advertising

The app shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google's advertising SDK may collect and process device information, including a device advertising identifier. In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the app asks for your consent before personalized ads are used; where consent is not given, ads are non-personalized.

Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can reset or delete your advertising identifier in your device settings.

Purchasing "Remove Ads" stops interstitial ads from being shown. Rewarded ads (which you choose to watch in exchange for hints) remain available.

3. Purchases

Purchases (Remove Ads and hint packs) are processed by Google Play Billing. The app receives confirmation that a purchase completed; it does not receive or store your payment details. Google's processing of purchases is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

The app does not sell your data. Data described above is shared only with Google in order to provide ads and process purchases.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is a general-audience puzzle game and is not directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new version will be posted at this URL.

7. Contact
