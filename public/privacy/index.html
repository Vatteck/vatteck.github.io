Privacy Policy

Continuity — Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. What Data We Collect

Nothing.

Continuity does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information whatsoever. There are no:

  • User accounts or sign-ins (no Google Sign-In, no email registration)
  • Analytics, crash reporting, or usage tracking SDKs
  • Advertising identifiers or ad networks
  • In-app purchases or payment processing
  • Location services, camera, microphone, or contacts access
  • Server backends — the game runs entirely on your device

2. Third-Party Services

Continuity integrates no third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. No data is shared with any external party because no data is collected in the first place.

3. Children's Privacy

Since the game collects no personal data, it is inherently compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. No age-gating or parental consent mechanisms are required.

4. Changes to This Policy

If future versions of Continuity introduce features that collect data (such as advertising or analytics), this privacy policy will be updated to reflect those changes before the new version is released. The current version will always be available at this URL.
